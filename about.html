THE PRIDE OF SIERRA LEONE

OUR VISION

To be the leading football agency in West Africa, discovering world-class talent and placing Sierra Leonean athletes on European fields.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in Freetown in 2010, we have grown from a community initiative to a force in the national football scene, backed by passionate supporters.

OUR MISSION

Providing holistic opportunities for youth to excel in sports and education, fostering discipline, teamwork, and national pride.
